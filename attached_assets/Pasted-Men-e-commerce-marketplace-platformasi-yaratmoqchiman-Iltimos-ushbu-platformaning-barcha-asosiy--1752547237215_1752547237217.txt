Men e-commerce (marketplace) platformasi yaratmoqchiman. Iltimos, ushbu platformaning barcha asosiy qismlarini, kichik modullarini, foydalanuvchi rollarini, texnik talablarini va muvaffaqiyatli ishlashini ta'minlovchi elementlarini to‘liq va chuqur tahlil qilib bering. Quyidagilarni har biri bo‘yicha aniq ro‘yxat, tavsif va texnik tavsiyalar keltiring:

Foydalanuvchi rollari (admin, seller, buyer, guest, support agent va boshqalar)

Foydalanuvchi interfeyslari (UI) (web, mobil, admin panel, seller panel)

Mahsulotlar va katalog tizimi (kategoriya, filtrlar, variantlar, narxlar, chegirmalar)

Buyurtma va checkout tizimi (savat, to‘lov tizimi, billing, shipping, order tracking)

To‘lov tizimlari integratsiyasi (Stripe, PayPal, Click, Payme, bank karta tizimlari)

Yetkazib berish va logistika (adreslar, shipment status, avtomatik hisoblash)

Qidiruv va tavsiya tizimi (AI-based recommendation, full-text search, filter)

Xavfsizlik (2FA, session management, rate limiting, SQL Injection, XSS, CSRF)

Mobil ilova imkoniyatlari (iOS/Android app uchun PWA, push notification, biometrik login)

Mahsulot sharhlari va reyting (moderatsiya, feedback, analytics)

Qo‘llab-quvvatlash tizimi (live chat, ticket system, FAQ, self-service)

Ko‘p tilli va ko‘p valyutali qo‘llab-quvvatlash (i18n, currency switch)

Marketing va analytics (email campaigns, Google Analytics, SEO, bannerlar, upsell/cross-sell)

Vendor management (seller registration, verification, payout, performance tracking)

Platformaning texnik arxitekturasi (microservices vs monolith, frontend-backend alohidalik, API Gateway, message queue)

CI/CD, test coverage va QA strategiyasi (unit test, integration test, regression test, test automation, staging environment)

Performance va scalability (load balancing, caching, database scaling, CDN)

Legal va compliance (GDPR, PCI DSS, refund policies, privacy policy)

Admin panel imkoniyatlari (dashboard, stats, user control, content management)

Future-proofing va extensibility (plugin system, modular architecture, open APIs)

Har bir band bo‘yicha aniq amaliy tavsiyalar, zamonaviy texnologiyalar (Next.js, NestJS, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Redis, RabbitMQ va boshqalar) asosida texnik yo‘nalishlar bilan tushuntirib bering.
